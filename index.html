Global Sports Insights

🇧🇷 Brazil – Football

5-time FIFA World Cup champions. Known for "Jogo Bonito" — the beautiful game.

🇺🇸 USA – Basketball

Birthplace of basketball. 16 Olympic gold medals in men's and women's events.

🇨🇳 China – Table Tennis

Dominant force since the 1950s. Consistently tops the world rankings.

🇵🇭 Philippines – Boxing

Home of Manny Pacquiao. World-class boxing heritage and growing athletic depth.

🇰🇪 Kenya – Marathon Running

High-altitude training has produced more marathon world records than any other nation.

🇯🇵 Japan – Martial Arts

Origin of Judo, Karate, and Aikido. 2021 Olympics host and top medal contender.
